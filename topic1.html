What does a nuclear Iran mean to regional political stability?

Hezbollah links

Iran and its government’s recurring links to Hezbollah serve as a red flag for nuclear help to ever be provided to Iran. Just last week, an Israeli investigation on an infiltrated Hezbollah base revealed that army generals had been sharing intelligence with the Hezbollah hierarchy. This coincides with Iran’s increasing state of economic turmoil since the plummet of oil prices. Being able to sell nuclear weaponry will certainly offset that. Giving nuclear resources such as enriched uranium and weapons grade plutonium to Iran is akin to giving nuclear weaponry to Hezbollah. Iran’s access to such materials raises the threat level.

In order for Iran to balance their budget they need oil to be priced over $136 per barrel. Oil is today being sold at $50 a barrel.
